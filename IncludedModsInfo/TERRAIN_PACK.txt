OTHER FEATURES
--------------
- Reworked Geoscape, including a new texture. 
- Country Zones have been redesigned to reflect better the country's geography
- North Atlantic, South Atlantic, Pacific and Indian Oceans can now have Alien Terror & Alien Research missions assigned. 
- Coastal cities have Port Attacks instead of vanilla Terror Sites.
- 8 new civilian units, some will only appear on certain terrains.
